About us

INFINIONAIRE PROPERTIES

Infinionaire Properties is part of Infinionaire Wealth Management Pty (Ltd). The Company specializes in Property Investment and App Development. The Company aims to connect Property owners with Investors and tenants by using modern digital technology , on every platform whether it is web related or mobile related. The Company was started in 2020 and with vast experience in Property , web and mobile development. The Company aims to bridge the gap in Africa's Technological and Property infrastructure. The company has seen the challenges faced in 2020 as a opportunity to grow and fully integrate with the modern requirements needed to survive in this difficult times. The Company will be helping other companies adapt to the modern requirements through Technology. Infinionaire Properties is an app that allows tenants to easily find properties and for home owners looking to sell their properties to be connected with investors. The app offers a simple user interface that is easy and simple to use.
